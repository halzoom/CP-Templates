1.The minimum vertex cover in a graph is a set of vertices such that
  every edge has at least one endpoint in the set.

2. The maximum independent  is a set of vertices such that no two
   chosen vertices are connected by an edge.

3. The maximum independent equal to the number of vertices - the minimum vertex cover

4. A global minimum cut of an undirected graph is the smallest number (or total weight)
   of edges whose removal disconnects the graph.
